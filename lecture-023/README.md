## Why do we need relational operators?

- To compare two values

## What are the relational operators?

- `==` - Equal to
- `!=` - Not equal to
- `>` - Greater than
- `<` - Less than
- `>=` - Greater than or equal to
- `<=` - Less than or equal to
- `!` - Logical NOT
- `&&` - Logical AND
- `||` - Logical OR
- `?:` - Ternary operator
